Our History

From one entrepreneurial vision to a growing multi-brand franchise network serving communities across the Philippines.

2010

Where It All Started

HHC Franchise Hub began with a small team and one clear goal: create practical negosyo opportunities for Filipino entrepreneurs through structured franchise systems and hands-on support.

Foundation Entrepreneur Support
2013

Early Traction and Trust

With strong market response, the team grew and the first operating playbooks were refined. This stage shaped the training and onboarding approach still used today.

Team Growth Operational Playbooks
2016

Expansion Across Business Formats

HHC accelerated into multiple franchise concepts, improving site selection, launch support, and day-to-day operations to help franchisees open with confidence.

Multi-Brand Growth Launch Support Field Execution
2019

Stronger Presence, Bigger Community

The organization expanded its footprint and strengthened franchisee partnerships, focusing on sustainable unit economics and consistent customer experience.

Network Expansion Franchisee Success
2022

Community and Responsible Growth

Beyond expansion, HHC deepened its focus on community impact, accessible business ownership, and long-term operational resilience for partners nationwide.

Community Programs Sustainable Growth
