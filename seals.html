Certified, registered, and trusted by industry authorities

HHC Franchise Hub is recognized and accredited by leading industry organizations, ensuring our franchisees receive world-class support and legitimate business opportunities. Our certifications reflect our commitment to transparency, compliance, and excellence.

Registered with the Department of Trade and Industry, ensuring our business operations meet all government standards and requirements for legitimate franchise operations.

SEC Registered

Officially registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission as a legitimate corporation, providing our partners with the assurance of a properly structured business entity.

Proud member of the Philippine Franchise Association, connecting us with the country's leading franchise community and ensuring adherence to industry best practices.

BIR Compliant

Fully compliant with Bureau of Internal Revenue requirements, ensuring all tax obligations are met and providing franchisees with proper documentation for their businesses.
